    <h2 class="text-uppercase mt-0">About <span class="text-theme">WNC</span></h2>
    <p class="text-theme fw-bold">Our ideology is based on Three S- Sampark, Sahyog and Sanrakshan. SAMPARK-We established contacts with the locals or concerned authorities. SAHYOG-To join us or to contribute, come together and SANRAKSHAN- Is to preserve or restore the apposite.</p>
    <p>We work with various organizations to understand the distributions, seasonality and abundance of birds.</p>
    <p>We believe, this ideology make us work in a more disciplined and transparent way. Our projects ranges from Wildlife protection, Wetland conservation, Awareness drive in rural and urban areas, Plantations and such kind of activities. The organization believes that environmental awareness act as a watchdog of environmental integrity.</p>
    <p class="text-theme fw-bold">We regularly organize lectures, Documentaries related with wildlife & environmental issues and conduct bird surveys.</p>
