WNC, Indore with the support from forest department at Kanha Tiger Reserve has completed the 7th bird survey from 13th -16th june 2024. FD KTR Shri Sk singh and DD core Shri Puneet Goyal concluded the event and rewarded every participants with a certificate. Survey saw participation of 83 volunteers from 15 different states. Along with the usual scientific survey activity 150 torch were donated for the forest guards and chaukidars as a part of welfare activity.

Some initial highlights of the survey : which are yet to be analysed are-
Brown wood owl, Emrald dove, Fork tail drongo cuckoo, Banded bay cuckoo, Bronze drongo, Spot bellied eagle owl.

Thanks to entire staff of kanha and all the participants for their time and efforts in this conservation journey.
