Who We Are

About WNC

Our ideology is based on Three S — Sampark, Sahyog and Sanrakshan.

Sampark

We establish contacts with the locals and concerned authorities.

Sahyog

To join us or to contribute, come together for a common cause.

Sanrakshan

To preserve or restore our natural heritage.

Our Work

What We Do

Wildlife Protection

From bird surveys to butterfly censuses, we work to understand and protect wildlife across Central India's forests and habitats.

Awareness & Education

We regularly organize lectures, documentaries, photo exhibitions, and workshops in schools and communities to spread conservation awareness.

Habitat Conservation

Wetland conservation, plantation drives, cleanliness campaigns, and environmental monitoring form the core of our on-ground activities.

We work with various organizations to understand the distributions, seasonality and abundance of birds. We believe this ideology makes us work in a more disciplined and transparent way.

Our projects range from Wildlife protection, Wetland conservation, Awareness drives in rural and urban areas, Plantations and such kind of activities. The organization believes that environmental awareness acts as a watchdog of environmental integrity.
