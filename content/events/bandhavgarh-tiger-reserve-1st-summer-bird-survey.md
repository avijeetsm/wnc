Its our please to announce the 1st bird survey-summer at Bandhavgarh Tiger Reserve. Forest Department has planned to conduct this survey with the objective to explore the bird diversity in various habitats of the park. This will help to establish key hotspots for further studies with a scientific approach. The management of Bandhavgarh Tiger Reserve has planned this exhaustive bird survey with organizing support from Wildlife & Nature Conservancy (WNC, Indore).

The survey will be conducted from 23rd to 26th May 2024. For more information, reach us on wncindore@gmail.com
