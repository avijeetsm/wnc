It is our pleasure to announce the bird survey at Panna Tiger Reserve. Forest Department has planned to conduct this survey with the objective to explore the bird diversity in various habitats of the park. This will help to establish key hotspots for further studies with a scientific approach. The management of Panna Tiger Reserve has planned this exhaustive Bird Survey with organizing and technical support from Wildlife & Nature Conservancy (WNC Indore).
