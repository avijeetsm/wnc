Who We Are

Wildlife & Nature
Conservancy Society

WNC is a nonprofit NGO established with an aim of working towards the cause of nature conservation. Based in Indore, the NGO works actively in the surrounding areas, concentrating on wildlife, environmental issues and creating awareness among the society.

Through presentations and campaigns in various institutions we make the coming generation sensitised and encourage them to find innovative solutions and methods to protect and conserve the nature.

What Drives Us

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To make mankind aware about the importance of nature and wildlife in its well being. Conserve wildlife along with its habitats with the help of maintaining existence data and spreading awareness amongst people. In partnership with communities and other organizations, WNC works towards achieving harmonious relationship between human beings and nature.

Our Vision

To conserve the nature and its flora and fauna with the help of dedicated nature warriors. We envision a future where wildlife thrives alongside human communities, where every citizen is a stakeholder in conservation, and where India's rich biodiversity is protected for generations to come.
